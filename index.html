Tim Hortons is a Canadian multinational fast-food restaurant chain known for its coffee, doughnuts, and other bakery products. It was founded by Tim Horton, a Canadian ice hockey player, and Jim Charade in 1964 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. The restaurant's name reflects the co-founder's name, Tim Horton.

Tim Hortons has also been involved in various charitable initiatives, and its "Tim Hortons Children's Foundation" works to help disadvantaged youth through various programs and camps.
